Hello, my name is Charles Severance. From may 1999 through January 2001, I was an Associate Director of the Media Union at the University of Michigan. I am the author of a book titled High Performance Computing from O'Reilly and Associates . My interests are in High Performance Computing, Visualization, Digital Media, Online Learning, TV and Radio.
 
For a number of years, I edited the Standards Column in the IEEE Computer Magazine . My favorite search engine is NorthernLight. A place to find stuff to buy is Shopper.
