JAVA was introduced in 1994 - It has been termed - “The most important Technology event after the Internet”. By 1994, journalists had all reported all about the “new internet” and gotten over it. In 1994, they were looking for the next story and JAVA popped up. It got a lot of hype and less than a year after its introduction, hundreds of books were written, colleges were teaching JAVA classes. A little over 500 days later, there were products which were based on this new language/operating environment.
