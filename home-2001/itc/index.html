Instructional Technology Committee


Note: This is a just a draft page under construction - it does NOT represent any particular consensus at this time and is intended for the private use of the committee members only. Any other rebroadcast...

The Instructional Technology Committee was formed in the Fall of 1999 and charged with finding a way to assess the instructional technology efforts across the College and develop a plan to enhance those efforts and the visibility of those efforts. The chair of the committee is Scott Fogler (sfogler@engin.umich.edu). The membership is drawn from various constituencies throughout the College.

Other Resources

This section points to other resources which may be useful to Engineering Faculty
  • The Teaching and Technology Collaborative works to connect faculty to services and resources on campus. They have an annual week-long event in the Spring of the year. In 2000, the event is May 8-12, 2000. The site also has a matrix of instructional technology resources on campus.

  • the Center for Research on Teaching and Learning is dedicated to supporting improvements in teaching and learning. These efforts range from workshops to course assessment programs.

  • UM Coursetools is a suite of tools for course management, communication, and management. Coursetools allows any instructor in any UMich class to quickly create a high quality website for the course. While coursetools is intended to be usable without significant training, workshops on its use are available.

  • Learning Technologies group which is part of the Media Union Faculty Studio provides support for Coursetools. As the rollout of Coursetools completes, the Learning Technologies group will begin to work with individual faculty on innovative uses of tools for teaching and learning.

Registry of Course Web Sites

The committee would like to maintain a list of courses which can serve as examples of good use of educational technology. Please send relavent URLs to the committee webmaster Charles Severance (csev@umich.edu) if you are involved in such a course.
