Federal Government agencies in the early 1990’s were under great pressure to demonstrate their value to the taxpayers. Gopher sites with “valuable” information for taxpayers which received a large number of hits were a great way for an agency to spport its claims for value to the taxpayers.
    One of the best early gopher examples - Michigan State Department of Education - School District MEAP tests
