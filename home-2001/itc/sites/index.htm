List of College of Engineering Web Technology Exemplar Sites

This list is intended to show some example sites using the web in innovative ways to teach. Please forward any additional URLs to sfogler@umich.edu.

Course-Related Sites

  • Eng 477 - Principles of Virtual Reality
    This course is taught once each year to students from a variety of majors. The course is taught using many technologies including the CAVE housed at the UM Media Union. Group projects from previous courses are available on the web site.

    Site contains: Course Materials, Reference Materials,

  • Matlab Modules - This site is a nationally used resource in the teaching of Matlab. The materials have been recently published in the form of a CD-ROM by Addison-Wesley. The design is to use Matlab in one window while viewing the materials in another window.

  • Demonstration On-Line Materials from the Center for Professional Development. This site has several examples of web-based lecture materials which have been used in support of various CPD courses. The materials are viewable without a password.

  • Chemical Reaction Engineering - This site is used to support ChE 344. In addition it provides asynchronous learning materials used around the country. The materials also support the textbook, Elements of Chemical Reaction Engineering. Portions of the on-line materials are augmented by a CD-ROM.

    Site Contents: Course materials, handouts, lecture materials, audio, and video.

  • ME360 This course uses CourseTools and requires a password to view the materials.

  • IOE474 - Modelling and Simulation.

    Site contains: Course materials, links to simulation resources, solutions to homework and labs, and other materials.

  • Eng 101 - Introduction to Computers and Programming

    Site contains: Course materials, background materials, solutions to homework and labs, and other materials.

  • NERS 554 - Radiation Shielding

    Site contains: Course materials, background materials, solutions to homework and labs, and other materials.

  • NERS441 - Nuclear Reactory Theory I

    Site contains: Exams, Suggested Reading, Homework Assignments and Solutions, and a Course Outline.

  • ME350 - Design and Manufacturing II

    Site contains: Course materials, background materials, solutions to homework and labs, and other materials.

  • ME558 - Discrete Design Optimization

    Site contains: Course materials, background materials, solutions to homework and labs, and other materials.
